Kay's famous boiled eggs

Ingredients

  • Eggs {4}
  • Water

Preparation

  1. Put all Eggs in a pot
  2. Fill pot with water until just above eggs
  3. Crank the heat on the stove
  4. Once boiling, reduce heat and boil for 4.5 minutes
  5. Remove from heat and shock with cold water
  6. Peel and eat

Notes

  • Not for the faint of heart
