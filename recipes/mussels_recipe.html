Mussels

Ingredients

  • Mussels {2 pounds}
  • White Wine {3/4 cup}
  • Red Pepper {1/2}
  • Garlic {1 clove}
  • Onion {1 medium}
  • Butter {2 tbsp}
  • Baguette

Preparation

  1. Clean mussels thoroughly, don't put them in water as this will kill them. Make sure that they all are shut (tap on the shell if open, if it's alive it will close)
  2. Dice onion, red pepper, and garlic
  3. Heat pot/wok on medium high and saute onion and pepper in butter until translucent
  4. Add garlic and saute until aromatic, about a half minute
  5. Crank heat to high and add white wine and mussels
  6. Cover and let steam until mussels open, about 5 or 6 minutes
  7. Add some more butter if you want, and serve fresh with sliced baguette

Notes

  • Buy the freshest mussels available, from a dependable fish market
  • Throw out any with cracked shells, or those that won't close
  • When cleaning, do your best to debeard the things
